When I said I had considered being a dive instructor, or a flight instructor, I was serious.
The appeal is not just in the adrenaline rush, or the ridiculous amount of gear I have to wear, or that tame hair is forever a thing of the past.

It's not just the cool photos (ok, that is a huge part of it) or the amazingly beautiful destinations.

The appeal is that my perspective was forever changed the moment I shed the confines of being a land-based human.
